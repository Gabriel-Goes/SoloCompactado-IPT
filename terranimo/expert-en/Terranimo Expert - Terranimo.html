Select machinery

Select the desired motor vehicle and machine in the left menu. In the submenu you can change the weights as desired. To adjust the tires, switch to the "Tires" menu on the far left.

Choose soil moisture

You can select between three moisture levels (dry, moist, wet). You can also enter values manually if you have measured data (e.g. tensiometer data). Enter your data either as matric potentials (in hPa or cbar) or as water content (%vol.).

Define soil

You can enter the soil properties in three ways: by selecting a standard soil type with the help of the soil type wizard, by using Google Maps to find your soil or by configuring your soil manually.The soil type wizard is the simplest way. If you define your soil manually, you can specify soil properties at each 10 cm-thick layers. PLEASE NOTE: Google Maps is not implemented at the moment.

Load share on tractor

Here you set the proportion of the weight that is transferred from the machine to the tractor. When the machine is fully lifted it is 100%, when using the traction control this can vary between values of 0% - 60%.

GPS

If the machinery is guided with a GPS, Terraniom reduces the overlapping area when calculating the area affected by compaction per hectare.
